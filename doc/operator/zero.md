## operator ZERO?

**ZERO?**


Status of zero flag. Used only within [.IF](../directive/dot_if.md), [.WHILE](../directive/dot_while.md), or [.REPEAT](../directive/dot_repeat.md) blocks and evaluated at run time, not at assembly time.
