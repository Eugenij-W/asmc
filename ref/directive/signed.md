## Signed compare

Flips the expression to signed if the first operand is a register.

.**IFS**_x_, **.WHILES**_x_, and **.UNTILS**_x_ <_expression_>.

- **S**
Signed REG

- **SB**
Signed AL

- **SW**
Signed AX

- **SD**
Signed EAX
