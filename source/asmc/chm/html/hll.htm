The HLL section

Asmc extensions are allowed in the following conditional control flow directives:

  • .IF expression
  • .IFD expression
  • .IFW expression
  • .IFB expression
  • .ELSEIF expression
  • .UNTIL expression
  • .WHILE expression
  • .BREAK .IF expression
  • .CONTINUE .IF expression
  • .SWITCH proc(...)
  • .CASE expression
  • .ENDC .IF expression
  • .ASSERT expression
  • .ASSERTD expression
  • .ASSERTW expression
  • .ASSERTB expression
